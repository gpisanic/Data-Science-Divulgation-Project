
Performance Improvement - Supply Chain

You will be responsible for managing one, or more, high-performing engagement teams at an executive level within the practice and the firm. You can expect to work with high-level client personnel to analyze, evaluate, and enhance specific functional areas within your specialized area of supply chain.

Skills And Attributes For Success

* Demonstrate in-depth technical capabilities and professional knowledge in your functional area of supply chain. Demonstrate ability to quickly learn new processes and uncover new offering that may assist current customer base.
* Provide mentoring and motivate diverse client engagement teams by delivering constructive on-the-job coaching to team members, while fostering an innovative and inclusive team-oriented work environment.
* Drive high-quality work within expected timeframes and on budget. Monitor progress, manage risk and ensure clients are kept informed about progress and expected outcomes.
* Build relationships with client personnel and deliver quality client services. Strive to assist our clients uncover additional areas of opportunity and manage the business development process.
* Understand EY and its service lines and actively assess what the firm can deliver to serve clients.
* Use your knowledge to mentor and lead a team of professionals on multiple projects

To qualify for the role you must have

* A bachelor’s degree or master’s degree
* About 8 years of experience in supply chain consulting environment
* Bring your expertise, focusing on logistics, strategic sourcing, manufacturing efficiency, planning, network design, logistics, managed services or supply chain analytics
* We would expect for you to be available to travel outside of their assigned office location at least 80% of the time. Successful candidates must work in excess of standard hours when necessary. A valid passport is required
* Develop to build a book of business and lead in the client proposal process

Ideally, you’ll also have

* A bachelor’s degree, a master’s degree focusing on engineering, supply chain, and/ or business
* Deep knowledge in one of the specific areas of expertise strategic sourcing, category management, warehouse and network design, manufacturing efficiency, product lifecycle management, demand planning and outsourcing advisory.
* Approximately 3 years of recent supply chain consulting experience


What We Look For

We’re looking for people that are passionate supply chain consultants, at the top of their game, but who would like to continue to grow their subject matter expertise and help others around them achieve their potential. If you have a passion for assisting businesses achieve their full potential and working with some of the highest performing teams, this role might very well be for you.
